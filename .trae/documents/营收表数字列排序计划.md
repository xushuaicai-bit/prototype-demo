# 营收3表数字列排序功能添加

## 摘要
- RevenueSummary.vue：已有 sortable，无需改动
- RevenueDetail.vue：13列需加 `sortable` 前端排序
- RevenueDeviation.vue：当月偏差表11列、季度偏差表8列需加 `sortable`

## 方案
使用 `sortable`（非 `sortable="custom"`），Element Plus 自动前端排序，无需额外事件处理函数。

### RevenueDetail.vue
- 13个数字列添加 `sortable`：
  - contractAmount, carryForwardRevenue, annualPlanRevenue, annualEstimatedRevenue, planAdjustmentRate
  - monthActualRevenue, annualAccumulatedRevenue, startAccumulatedRevenue
  - monthReportedRevenue, annualReportedRevenue, totalReportedRevenue, remainingContractAmount
  - progress

### RevenueDeviation.vue - 当月偏差表
- 补充6个无prop列的prop属性：monthPlanRevenue, monthActualRevenue, completionRate, deviation, negativeDeviation
- 11个数字列添加 `sortable`

### RevenueDeviation.vue - 季度偏差表
- 8个数字列添加 `sortable`：contractAmount, carryForwardRevenue, annualPlanRevenue, annualAccumulatedRevenue, totalAccumulatedRevenue, quarterOriginalPlan, quarterRevisedPlan, planReductionAmount

## 验证
点击3个表中的数字列表头，确认出现排序箭头且能升序/降序切换
